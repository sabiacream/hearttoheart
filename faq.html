Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers about readings, payment, appointments, and restoring a past pull.

How much is a reading?

Online readings are currently $5 and are unlocked after PayPal checkout.

How do I restore a previous reading?

Go to Restore Reading and enter your PayPal Transaction ID from your receipt email.

Do I need an account?

No account is required. Keep your Transaction ID safe so you can restore your reading on any device.

Are readings refundable?

Readings are digital and personalized, so all sales are final once unlocked.

Can I get an in-person reading?

Yes. Call (610) 762-8341 or (267) 664-2928 to ask about in-person sessions and availability at the Main Street studio in Coopersburg.

Do you offer more than tarot?

Yes. The studio also offers psychic and medium readings, Reiki, chakra sessions, pendulum readings, and package deals.

Are virtual readings available?

Virtual readings may be available upon request for certain services. The best way to ask is by phone or Facebook message.

What are the gift shop hours?

Gift shop hours may change seasonally, so the best place to check current hours and walk-in updates is the Heart to Heart 111 Facebook page.
